Genetic misunderstood

December 26, 2019

Most of the ordinary people do not know about the genes and chromosomes. Definition of genetic literacy presented by Bowling is “sufficient knowledge and appreciation of genetic principles to allow informed decision-making for personal well-being and effective participation in social decisions on genetic issues”. There are other studies exploring public understading of genetics. Dr Michael Dougherty has several papers discussing about the chanlleges of public education about genetics. His studies are focued on USA educational system.

Why sharing photo?!

December 23, 2019

Obtaining informed consent for photo sharing is one of the challenges that genetic researchers in counteries in MENA region encounter. Most of the parents are reluctant to share their child’ photo. The traditional formats of manuscripts - writing about participants in Material and methods section - have led to the wrong culture among the researchers to objectify patients. I witnesses times that the patient’s guardians were handed a consent form explaining the usage of the sample and confidentiality of the patient information including data and photos.

good collaboration

December 21, 2019

Many institutes have policies and guidelines for collborative studies. What other criteria research leaders consider when starting a collaborative studies? There are some criteria in a paper published by Professor Michael Parker in 2016. Here a brief note is provided. 1- Engaging the collaborators in the study conception and the intellectual work. 2- Requesting for the information about the patients and high-quality samples according to a timetable. 3- Ensuring high quality research with excellent proposal.
